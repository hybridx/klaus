## Web Research

Systematic approach to researching a topic and producing a well-sourced summary.

### Procedure

1. **Clarify the question** — identify exactly what information is needed
2. **Search broadly** — use multiple queries with different keywords
3. **Evaluate sources** — prefer official docs, peer-reviewed content, reputable sites
4. **Cross-reference** — verify claims across at least two independent sources
5. **Take structured notes** — organize findings by sub-topic
6. **Synthesize** — combine findings into a coherent summary
7. **Cite sources** — include URLs or references for all key claims
